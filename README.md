## About These Essays

These essays explore the use of space tethers for orbital momentum exchange 
(MXER — Momentum eXchange/Electrodynamic Reboost) as a propellantless 
means of orbital transfer and spacecraft reboosting.

They were written in connection with a non-degree continuing education course 
in orbital mechanics at Johns Hopkins University. They have previously been 
published on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com) and 
[Substack](https://www.substack.com) and are shared here for structured 
peer review and comment by fellow students and colleagues in the field.
